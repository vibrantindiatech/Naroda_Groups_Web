Rental Guide & Tips

1
Budget Planning

Consider all costs: security deposit (usually 2-3 months rent), monthly rent, maintenance charges, and utilities. Plan for 25-30% of your monthly income for rent.

2
Documentation

Prepare necessary documents: ID proof, address proof, employment proof, and photographs. Read the rental agreement carefully before signing.

3
Property Inspection

Check all amenities, water supply, electrical fittings, and plumbing. Take photos/videos before moving in for reference during security deposit return.

Quick Tip

Always negotiate the rent and security deposit. Most landlords are open to reasonable negotiations, especially for longer lease terms.
