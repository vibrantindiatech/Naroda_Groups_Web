Senior Sales Manager

Ahmedabad Full-time 8+ Years Experience
Job Description

We are looking for an experienced Senior Sales Manager to lead our sales team and drive revenue growth for our premium residential and commercial projects.

Responsibilities
  • Develop and implement sales strategies to achieve business targets
  • Lead, mentor, and manage the sales team
  • Build and maintain relationships with key clients and stakeholders
  • Monitor market trends and competitor activities
  • Prepare sales reports and forecasts for management
  • Collaborate with marketing team on promotional activities
Requirements
  • MBA in Marketing or related field (preferred)
  • 8+ years of sales experience in real estate
  • Proven track record of achieving sales targets
  • Strong leadership and team management skills
  • Excellent communication and negotiation skills
  • Knowledge of Ahmedabad real estate market
What We Offer
  • Competitive salary with attractive commission structure
  • Performance bonuses and incentives
  • Health insurance and other benefits
  • Opportunities for career advancement
  • Professional development programs

Site Engineer

Ahmedabad Full-time 3+ Years Experience
Job Description

We are seeking a skilled Site Engineer to supervise construction activities and ensure quality control at our project sites.

Responsibilities
  • Supervise day-to-day construction activities on site
  • Ensure quality control and adherence to specifications
  • Coordinate with contractors, architects, and project managers
  • Monitor safety standards and compliance
  • Maintain project documentation and reports
  • Resolve technical issues on site
Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering
  • 3+ years of construction site experience
  • Knowledge of construction methods and materials
  • Proficiency in AutoCAD and MS Office
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities
What We Offer
  • Competitive salary package
  • Site allowances and benefits
  • Opportunities for skill development
  • Career growth in project management
  • Health and safety equipment provided
