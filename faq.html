Booking Process Questions

The booking process is simple and transparent:

  1. Site Visit: Schedule a site visit to experience the project firsthand
  2. Unit Selection: Choose your preferred unit based on availability
  3. Booking Amount: Pay the initial booking amount (typically 10% of property value)
  4. Agreement: Sign the booking agreement and receive allotment letter
  5. Documentation: Submit required documents for verification
  6. Payment Plan: Choose a suitable payment plan as per construction milestones
  7. Loan Processing: We assist with home loan processing if required
  8. Possession: Receive possession upon project completion and final payment

Note: Our sales team will guide you through each step and ensure a smooth booking experience.

You will need the following documents for booking a property:

For Individuals:

  • Identity Proof (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Passport, or Driving License)
  • Address Proof (Aadhar Card, Passport, Utility Bills, or Bank Statement)
  • Passport-sized photographs (2 copies)
  • PAN Card copy (mandatory for registration)
  • Income proof for loan applicants

For Companies:

  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • Board Resolution authorizing the purchase
  • PAN Card of the company
  • Authorized signatory's ID and address proof

Additional Documents (if applicable):

  • Power of Attorney (if booking through representative)
  • NRI documents (for NRIs)
  • Previous property documents (for resale properties)

Yes, we offer multiple options for booking:

Online Booking:

  • You can initiate the booking process through our website
  • Fill the online enquiry form with your details
  • Our sales representative will contact you within 24 hours
  • Virtual site tours available for remote booking
  • Online document submission facility

Offline Booking:

  • Visit our sales office at Naroda-Bhat Road, Ahmedabad
  • Contact our sales team to schedule a site visit
  • Complete the booking process at our office

Recommendation: We recommend visiting the site in person or taking a virtual tour before making a booking decision. Our team is available to assist you through both online and offline channels.

Payments & Finance Questions

We offer flexible payment plans to suit different financial needs:

Construction-Linked Plan (Most Popular):

  • 10% at time of booking
  • 10% within 30 days of booking
  • Balance 80% linked to construction milestones
  • Payments spread over project duration

Down Payment Plan:

  • 20% down payment at time of booking
  • Balance 80% at time of possession
  • Suitable for buyers with immediate funds

Flexi Payment Plan:

  • Customizable payment schedule
  • Tailored to individual financial situations
  • Extended payment timelines available

Subvention Scheme (with Bank Tie-up):

Legal & RERA Questions

Yes, all our ongoing and upcoming projects are registered under RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Authority).

RERA registration ensures:

  • Transparency in project details and pricing
  • Adherence to promised timelines
  • Proper utilization of customer funds in escrow accounts
  • Legal protection for homebuyers
  • Quality assurance as per approved plans

Our RERA Registration Details:

  • Naroda Landmark: RERA No. GUJRERA/12345/2023
  • Naroda Irish: RERA No. GUJRERA/67890/2023
  • All registration details are displayed at our sales office and project sites
  • Registration certificates are provided to all customers

Why RERA Matters: RERA registration provides legal protection and ensures that developers deliver projects as promised. It's your assurance of a safe and secure real estate investment.

We provide comprehensive legal documentation for complete transparency:

During Booking:

  • Booking Application Form
  • Allotment Letter
  • Payment Receipts
  • Copy of RERA Registration
  • Approved Building Plans

During Agreement:

  • Agreement for Sale (with all schedules)
  • Payment Plan Schedule
  • Possession Timeline
  • Specification Sheet

At Possession:

  • Sale Deed/Conveyance Deed
  • Occupancy Certificate (if applicable)
  • Completion Certificate
  • No Objection Certificates (NOCs)
  • Maintenance Agreement
  • Society Share Certificate (if applicable)

Legal Verification: We recommend that buyers get all documents verified by their own legal advisor. We provide all necessary documents in advance for this purpose.
