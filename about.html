Our Story

Building Dreams, Creating Landmarks

Founded in 2008, Naroda Group has been at the forefront of Ahmedabad's real estate landscape. What began as a small family business has grown into one of the city's most trusted real estate developers, with a portfolio spanning commercial, residential, and mixed-use developments.

Our journey is built on three core principles: Quality, Transparency, and Customer Satisfaction. These values have guided us through 50+ successful projects and have helped us create homes and workspaces for thousands of families and businesses.
