Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our properties and services

You can schedule a site visit by filling out our contact form, calling our sales team directly, or using the WhatsApp button on our website. Our representatives will coordinate with you to arrange a convenient time for the visit.

For property booking, you'll need identity proof (Aadhar card, passport, or driver's license), address proof, PAN card, passport-size photographs, and booking amount cheque/DD. Our sales team will provide you with a complete checklist.

Yes, all our ongoing and upcoming projects are registered under RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Authority). We provide RERA registration numbers for each project and ensure complete transparency in all our dealings.

We offer flexible payment plans including construction-linked plans, down payment plans, and EMI options. We also have special schemes for NRIs and corporate bookings. Our financial advisors can help you choose the best plan based on your requirements.

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our sales numbers directly. You'll receive an acknowledgment email immediately after form submission.
