Property Description

Welcome to this stunning 3 BHK luxury apartment at Naroda Irish, offering 1500 sq.ft. of elegantly designed living space. This north-facing apartment on the 5th floor provides abundant natural light and beautiful views of the landscaped gardens and cityscape.

The apartment features a spacious living room with large windows, a modern modular kitchen with premium fittings, three well-appointed bedrooms with attached wardrobes, and two luxurious bathrooms with high-quality sanitaryware. The master bedroom includes a walk-in closet and an ensuite bathroom.
